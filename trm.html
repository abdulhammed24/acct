Tax and Regulatory Matters

Based on historical fact, tax is one of the key costs in a business, and as such it should be properly managed in a way that the inherent risks in a company’s tax position are properly monitor and controlled.

Even in today's global market place, tax presents many problems for international business. Tax regimes are complex and vary from country to country, while tax laws continue to change annually. Expert tax advice is therefore vital wherever you do business.

We provide proactive tax management service that helps our firms’ clients to eliminate any additional tax exposure in form of penalties and interests, improve relationships with Tax Office, hence place control of the tax management process in the hands of the Tax Payers, and not the Tax Office.

Key Services includes:

  • Preparation of filings of tax returns
  • Obtaining tax clearance certificate
  • General advice on compliance matters
  • Certificate of acceptance services
  • Tax Planning
  • Tax health check
  • Withholding tax compliance
  • Tax dispute resolution
  • Tax audit support
  • Value added tax compliance
  • Tax risk management
