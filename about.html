about us

JOHN GRACEWELL CONSULTING is a highly professional consulting firm that provides specialised services tailored to individual client’s needs.

We provide world-class analysis and clear solutions in Accounting, Tax and Regulatory Matter, Fraud Risk Management, Forensics Accounting/Audit, Fraud Investigation, Outsourced Internal Audit and Business services for companies around …………

We work closely with our clients to anticipate and adapt to changes in the regulatory environment and assist in putting in place better programs and control to address the vast variety of regulations and regulatory risks.

We also assist our clients to effectively identify and prioritize areas of fraud risk within their organisation, with a focus on the review of potential fraud schemes and the internal controls in place to prevent or detect these schemes.

We provide proactive tax management service that helps our clients to eliminate any additional tax exposure in form of penalties and interests, improve relationships with Tax Office, hence place control of the tax management process in the hands of the Tax Payers, and not the Tax Office. As Tax Consultants, we keep abreast on-going legislative and regulatory changes and new case that give rise to changes in application and interpretation of tax laws.

We offer outsourced internal audit service that enables our firms’ clients to maximise the productive and profitable use of their business resources and ensures compliance with laws and regulations that affects the organisation.

As a team, we have more than 100 years of combined experience as Qualified Accountants, Internal Auditors, External Auditors, Forensic Accountants, Certified Information System Auditors, Business and Administrative Managers and Certified Fraud Examiners.
