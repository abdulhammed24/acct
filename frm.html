Fraud Risk Management

Fraud is extremely costly to organisations and economies, often resulting in the bankruptcy of companies. Statistics that are available suggest that fraud is increasing in both amount and frequency and as a result experienced business leaders need to put in place strategies that will mitigate these fraud and misconduct risks.

John Gracewell Consulting fraud risk management services assist our firms’ clients to effectively identify and prioritize areas of fraud risk within their organisation, with a focus on the review of potential fraud schemes and the internal controls in place to prevent or detect these schemes. We advise our firms’ clients on programs that incorporate policies and procedures designed to prevent, detect, and respond to fraud.
